Winter is coming...did you bring your coat?

Submitted by Bryce Rasmussen on Wed, 11/28/2012 - 02:21

[The following is a cross-post from my personal blog.]

 

Get excited. Winter in Minnesota. I love it! This past week has been great. Cold, cold, but great. The week before was too warm. I was able to ride my Specialized Tarmac every day to work last week - in late November. It all changed on Thanksgiving. The day started out like it had the days previous, warm, and by the time pie was being digested it started snowing pretty hard. It was finally time to hang the crabon in the basement and break out the studly steel bad boy.

My All-City Space Horse soaking in the snow filled sunshine.

I love this bike. It's just the bee's knees. I strip all the accessories off for some serious gravel speed, or load it down with 40mm studded tires for winter. I have a pair of Schwalbe Marathon Winters which are entering into their fourth season of use. They definitely show signs of wear, but after three years of use are still just as confidence inspiring as ever, and heavy....very, very heavy. Look close in the photo below and you can see the inner studs are slightly worn.

Getting worn, but who can complain with four years use for $75?

Looking ahead in the forecast next week looks to be rather mild. Maybe mild enough that the Tarmac will get some more miles before it's really done for the year. 

 

On another note Tim and I had a cold and wet bike date in early October and here's a few glamour shots.

Tim being fast.

Us being happy.

Enjoy the last of November!
